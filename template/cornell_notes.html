Cornell Notes Template

title

Reduce

  • Summarize facts, ideas, concepts
  • Known as cue section

Record

  • Record as many meaningful facts, ideas, concepts as you can

Summary

  • You can write reflection, summary, or goals
  • review and recite as needed

Recite

  • Cover record section
  • Use only reduce section
  • Say over the facts and ideas of the lecture as fully as you can but not mechanically and in your own words
  • verify what you have said

Reflect

  • Consider how this material relates to the course and unit/subject being discussed
  • Important part of learning process

Review

  • Spend 10 minutes every week in quick review of your notes
  • You will retain most of what you have learned
